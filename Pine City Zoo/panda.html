PANDA

Pandas are large bears native to China, belonging to the family Ursidae and the species Ailuropoda melanoleuca. They are known for their distinctive black and white fur and primarily feed on bamboo. Pandas inhabit the mountainous regions of central China.

Pandas are herbivorous, with bamboo making up 99% of their diet. They spend most of their day eating and can consume up to 38 kg (84 lb) of bamboo daily. Pandas are solitary animals and are known for their playful behavior.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the pandas at pen G8, in the bamboo forest exhibit.

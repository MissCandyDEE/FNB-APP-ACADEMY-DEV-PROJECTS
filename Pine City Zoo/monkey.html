MONKEY

Monkeys are primates belonging to the infraorder Simiiformes, which includes a wide variety of species found in tropical regions around the world. They are known for their agility, intelligence, and social behavior. Monkeys can be found in Central and South America, Africa, and Asia.

Monkeys are omnivorous, feeding on fruits, nuts, seeds, and insects. They are highly social animals, often living in troops and exhibiting complex social structures. Monkeys are known for their playful behavior and ability to use tools.

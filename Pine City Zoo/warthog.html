WARTHOG

Warthogs are wild pigs native to Africa, belonging to the family Suidae and the species Phacochoerus africanus. They are characterized by their tusks and facial warts. Warthogs are primarily found in savannahs and grasslands across sub-Saharan Africa.

Warthogs are omnivorous, feeding on grasses, roots, and fruits. They are known for their unique way of moving, often running on their knuckles. Warthogs are social animals, often seen in small groups or family units.
